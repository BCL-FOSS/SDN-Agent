About

A unconventional tech company comes with an unconventional backstory

  • 2013-2024

    Learning & Growing

    An early start at a Queens, NY based Managed Services Provider laid the foundation for a career spent designing, deploying & managing IT infrastructure for various companies. The following roles were held at several entities within the Managed Services, Product Engineering, Federal Government and Higher Education industries: Datacenter Technician, Linux/Systems Administrator, Systems Engineer, Security Analyst, IT Specialist.

  • May 2024

    A New Hope

    After 10+ years delivering IT solutions, several areas were identified as hindrances to the actual experience of managing networks. In a word, it's painful. Tools for monitoring, automation and backups are either tedious to implement or have complicated pricing, preventing small IT teams from managing mid-sized to large networks with ease.

    Frustrated with the lack of innovation and clear focus on profit maximization by many service providers, Baugh Consulting & Lab (B.C. & L) L.L.C. was formed to provide Monitoring, Automation & Business Continuity software that allows IT Professionals to manage networks of any size with ease. Along with operations as a SaaS provider, B.C. & L also provides IT consulting and Co-Managed Services.

  • May 2024 - April 2025

    Building the future of IT

    Baugh Consulting & Lab launches its Network Monitoring, Automation & Business Continuity SaaS platform, umjiniti.

  • Present

    Going Global

    Baugh Consulting & Lab L.L.C. continues to stay at the forefront of innovations within tech, provide top-notch services to clients, and looks to expand to emerging markets throughout the globe.
